Recipe for Dal Bhat taken from:  
http://www.yowangdu.com/tibetan-food/dal-bhat.html

Ingredients:
•	1 cup red lentils
•	1 tbsp vegetable oil
•	1 small red onion, diced
•	2 cloves garlic, minced
•	1 tbsp ginger, minced
•	¾ tsp salt
•	½ tsp mustard seeds
•	½ tsp turmeric
•	½ tsp cumin seeds
•	½ tsp coriander powder
•	1 medium tomato, diced
•	2 tbsp cilantro / green onion
•	Water, to make soup
Preparation:
•	Wash lentils several times and soak (up to overnight to improve cooking time)
•	Stir-fry ginger, garlic, onion for 1-2 min on high heat
•	Stir in cumin seeds, salt, turmeric, mustard seed, coriander powder. (turn heat down to 6/10 and cook for 2 minutes)
•	Add tomatoes and covered cook 4 minutes
•	Add lentils and covered cook 5 minutes.
•	Add 1 cup water and cover cook 5 minutes.  (In large sauce pan)
•	Stir in 2 more cups of water
•	Continue Cooking on medium heat for 10 minutes.
•	Dal should be ready (Opt. top with cilantro / green onion)
